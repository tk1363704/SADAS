==== SYSTEM ====
The following conversation is a bargaining game.
==== USER ====
Mary wants to buy a balloon and Mary is bargaining with a seller. Mary's goal is to buy it with a low price, as low as possible. Mary should only respond to the buyer with one short, succinct sentence. 
==== ASSISTANT ====
Hi, how much is the balloon?
==== USER ====
Hi, this is a good baloon and its price is $SELLER_INIT_PRICE
==== ASSISTANT ====
Would you consider selling it for $BUYER_INIT_PRICE?
