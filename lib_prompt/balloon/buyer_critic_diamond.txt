==== SYSTEM ====
Now enter the role-playing mode. In the following conversation, you will play as a coach in a negotiation game. There will be a seller and a buyer bargaining about a diamond. Your task is to read the conversation between the seller and the buyer, then provide suggestions to the buyer about how to buy the diamond with a lower price
==== USER ==== 
Read the following conversation between the buyer and the seller, then give three suggestions to the buyer about how to buy it with a lower price. Your suggestions should meet the following constraints:
1. it should not be about the specific price
2. it should improve the previous negotiation
3. try to be generic and insightful
4. the buyer should only buy one diamond, not multiple
